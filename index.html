Xueqing Wu
(Xueqing is pronounced as Shweh-Ching)

I am a second-year PhD student at UCLA, advised by Prof. Kai-Wei Chang and Prof. Nanyun Peng. Preivously, I received my master's degree from UIUC in 2023 advised by Prof. Heng Ji, and my bachelor's degree from USTC in 2020. I have work experience in IBM Research, Bytedance AI Lab and MSRA.

Research: My research interests focus on multi-modal reasoning, such as grounding, self-reflection, and reasoning enhanced by code generation.
